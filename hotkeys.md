# Hotkeys
Add the hotkeys on `brave://settings/system/shortcuts`.

## Tab
- New Tab: `Ctrl`+`T`
- Reopen Closed Tab: `Ctrl`+`Shift`+`T`
- Close Tab: `Ctrl`+`W`
- Back: `Ctrl`+`H`
- Next Tab: `Ctrl`+`J`
- Previous Tab: `Ctrl`+`K`
- Forward: `Ctrl`+`L`
- 
## Page
- Reload: `Ctrl`+`R`
- Reload bypassing cache: `Ctrl`+`Shift`+`r`
- 
## Miscellaneous
- Copy URL: `Ctrl`+`B`
